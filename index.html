School Classroom

At VIBGYOR, we ensure that the process of assimilating knowledge works seamlessly. For this very purpose, we have designed our classrooms to be comfortable and complementary to our learning methods. All classrooms are air-conditioned and complete with learning resources.

School Library

An open access library provides an all-encompassing opportunity for reading, reference and research to all age groups.

School science lab

Observation, hypothesising and experimentation are a crucial part of development in the field of the Natural Sciences. Our Science labs provide ample opportunity for the children to develop their skills through alternative techniques. The labs are interactive spaces that allow children to grow laterally, through an environment outside the classroom.

What's New

  • First Virtual Assembly

    Middle School struck the right note by beginning the Virtual Assembly on the theme - Peace. The idea received an exhilarating response and support from the learners who were more than excited to join their first virtual assembly after a long gap. Children enacted short skits on peace displaying their colorful virtual backgrounds and ingeniously made props. Many shared wise thoughts on peace whereas some sang songs.


  • VvMUN

    The VvMUN is a programme that is looked forward to by students and this year it was a great success even in these trying times. The two days virtual session gave a lot to the students in terms of knowledge, confidence, and friendship. The feeling of self-importance and being rewarded for the participation took them a level higher. The research done and the new concepts learnt opened a new world for them. It has helped many students to develop leadership skill, research, writing, public speaking and problem-solving skills.
